Title: Minotaur Puzzle levels
Puzzles: minotaur
Themes: modern1, moss, minecraft, classic, ancient2, modern2, default
Main: 1

This video shows a gameplay of 10 Minotaur Puzzle levels, mostly
autogenerated, but also 2 pre-created. With the Minotaur making 2, 3 or 1
moves per turn. Both single-room levels and multi-room levels. It also
shows these features: Defeat and **level reload** (*Alt-R*), Undo (*u*),
theme switch (*F1*) and auto-solving (*KP Enter* twice).
