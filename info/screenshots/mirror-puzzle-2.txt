Title: Mirror Reflectivity level
Puzzle: mirror
Theme: ancient2

This shows Reflectivity level where you need to toggle the mirrors'
activeness to direct the beam from the generator in the top-left corner
to the collector in the center. The mirrors' orientations are fixed.

This level is logically the same as
[Mazelog Reflectivity level by David Johnson-Davies](http://www.mazelog.com/show?179).
