Title: Mirror Sunbeams level
Puzzle: mirror
Theme: classic

This shows Sunbeams level where you need to toggle the mirrors' activeness
to direct the beam from the generator in the center to the collector in
the bottom-right corner. The mirrors' orientations are fixed.

This level is logically the same as
[Mazelog Sunbeams level by David Johnson-Davies](http://www.mazelog.com/show?1C2).
